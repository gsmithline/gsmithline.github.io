I'm originally from the Philadelphia area. For undergrad I attended Lafayette College where I studied Statistics, Economics, and Data Science in 2021. There I also was a D1 athlete where I was on an athletic scholarship, was named a captain, was all conference, and was an all-american lacrosse player. After Lafayette I was working as a software engineer and attending Tufts University to pursue my MS in Computer Science, where I had a great experience, got to work on interesting research, and met many great people who allowed me to be a student while I worked in industry; I graduated from Tufts in 2024. In fall 2024 I will be pursuing my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Michigan Ann Arbor, where I will be joining the Strategic Reasoning Group within the AI Lab there.

Research Interests
